# Getting Started with AxCouncil

This guide walks you through your first 10 minutes with AxCouncil.

---

## Step 1: Sign Up (30 seconds)

1. Go to [axcouncil.com](https://axcouncil.com)
2. Click **Sign in with Google** or enter your email
3. Verify your email if prompted
4. You're in!

---

## Step 2: Your First Council Query (2 minutes)

Let's ask the council a question to see how it works.

### Try This Example

Copy and paste this into the input bar:

```
I'm launching a new productivity app. What are the 3 biggest mistakes
first-time founders make when launching, and how can I avoid them?
```

Press **Enter** or click **Send**.

### Watch the 3 Stages

1. **Stage 1** (15-20s): Five AI models appear and start responding
2. **Stage 2** (10-15s): Models review each other's responses
3. **Stage 3** (5-10s): Chairman synthesizes the final answer

### Read Your Results

- Each AI model's individual response is in a collapsible card
- The **Best Answer** at the bottom combines the best insights
- Click **Copy** to copy the synthesis

---

## Step 3: Add Your Company (3 minutes)

Personalize responses by adding your company context.

### Create Your Company

1. Click the **building icon** in the input bar
2. Select **+ Add Company**
3. Fill in:
   - **Name**: Your company name
   - **Industry**: What sector you're in
   - **Context**: Describe your business (see below)

### Example Company Context

```markdown
## About Us
TechStart is a B2B SaaS startup building project management tools
for remote teams. Founded 2024, based in Austin, TX.

## Stage & Size
- Pre-seed, raised $500K
- Team of 5 (2 engineers, 1 designer, 1 sales, 1 founder)
- 50 beta users, targeting 1,000 by Q2

## Key Challenges
- Limited runway (12 months)
- Competing with established players (Asana, Monday)
- Need to find product-market fit fast

## Constraints
- Can't hire more until Series A
- Focus on SMB market initially
- Must be self-serve, low-touch sales
```

### Save and Select

1. Click **Save**
2. Now select your company from the dropdown in the input bar
3. All future queries will include this context!

---

## Step 4: Try a Contextual Query (2 minutes)

With your company selected, ask something specific:

```
Given our limited runway and team size, should we focus on
building more features or improving our current onboarding
experience? What metrics should drive this decision?
```

**Notice the difference**: The council now considers your specific situation, constraints, and goals.

---

## Step 5: Explore the Interface (2 minutes)

### Sidebar (Left)
- **Conversations**: Your chat history
- **Search**: Find past queries
- **Star**: Mark important chats
- **Filter**: Show starred/archived only

### Input Bar (Bottom)
- **Company icon**: Select company context
- **Department icon**: Focus on a department
- **Playbook icon**: Add specific playbooks
- **Mode toggle**: Switch between Council and Chat

### Top Navigation
- **My Company**: Manage context, playbooks, decisions
- **Settings**: API keys, preferences
- **Theme toggle**: Light/dark mode

## Quick Tips

| Tip | Why |
|-----|-----|
| Be specific | More context = better answers |
| Use Council for big decisions | Use Chat for quick follow-ups |
| Save good responses | Build your knowledge base |
| Add playbooks | Teach the council your processes |
| Check different AI responses | Sometimes the "minority" view is gold |

**Q: Is this using my OpenAI/Anthropic account?**
