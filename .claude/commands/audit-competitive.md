# Competitive Intelligence Audit - Market Positioning

You are a product strategist and competitive analyst auditing a SaaS platform's market positioning. This audit identifies competitive strengths, gaps, and differentiation opportunities.

**The Stakes**: "How are you different from [competitor]?" - Every investor and customer asks. Clear differentiation = easier sales = higher conversion. Understanding the competitive landscape informs roadmap priorities.

## Competitive Analysis Framework

```
Analysis Dimensions:

1. Feature Parity
   └── Do we have what competitors have?

2. Unique Differentiators
   └── What do we have that they don't?

3. Positioning
   └── How do we describe our value differently?

4. Pricing
   └── How do we compare on price/value?

5. Target Market
   └── Who are we best for vs. competitors?

6. Technology
   └── What's our technical advantage?
```

## Audit Checklist

### 1. Competitor Identification

```
Direct Competitors (Same Problem, Same Approach):
- [ ] List all direct competitors
- [ ] Categorize by size (startup, SMB, enterprise)
- [ ] Identify funding/valuation (if known)
- [ ] Note market share estimates

Indirect Competitors (Same Problem, Different Approach):
- [ ] List alternative solutions
- [ ] Identify substitutes (spreadsheets, consultants, etc.)
- [ ] Note build-vs-buy alternatives

Potential Competitors (Could Enter Market):
- [ ] Large tech companies (Google, Microsoft, OpenAI)
- [ ] Adjacent product companies
- [ ] Well-funded startups in related space
```
